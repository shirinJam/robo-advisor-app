Approach

Our Approach

Our approach is based on leveraging the power of AI and fundamental Finance to keep our investors one step ahead.

Principle

RoboFina focuses on increasing expected return wihtin appropriate level of risk by balancing emotions with your demographic attributes

Principle

RoboFina make sure no investor is highly influenced by emotions and at the same time it takes in account the extend of risk an investor can bear by carefully analyzing his behavior. Our system takes into account an investors behavior as well as demographic aspects in order to calculate an appropriate risk tolerance score using sophisticated mathematical models.

Process

RoboFina performs strategic asset allocation based on the leading financial algorithms in the industry

Process

Once the risk tolerance of an individual investor is calculated, Robofina performs asset allocation based on the algorithms inspired from award winning research. At the same time, Robofina makes sure the investor always stays within his risk tolerance bounds.

Portfolio

RoboFina diversifies your portfolio across various ranges of ETFs available worldwide

Portfolio

Robofina focuses on passively managed funds i.e. investment in ETFs. We have carefully handpicked ETF’s that will provide highest level of diversification across many asset classes, at the same time providing high returns.

Vision

Our Vision

Investing is not difficult and everyone should have equal apportunities to invest. RoboFina strives to make the investment procedure as easy as possible so that inexperienced investors and beginners can make use of this platform to start their journey into the investment world.

We also believe in educating our investors by giving them uptpdate information about market trends and suggestions and keeping our processes transparent, so when the time comes our investors are confident enough to switch to the premium package where they are free to form their own portfolios based on their knowledge as well as our support an suggestions.
